# Real-Time Property Investment Calculator

An interactive calculator for comparing buying and renting with live financial projections.

## Why I built it

I wanted a clearer way to explore realistic housing scenarios instead of static spreadsheet-style comparisons.

## Focus

- Real-time updates as assumptions change
- Clear visualization of longer-term outcomes
- A fast front-end experience that makes the trade-offs easier to understand

## What it shows

The project is mainly a front-end and state-management exercise: taking financial inputs, recalculating the model immediately, and presenting the result in a way that stays understandable as the numbers change.
