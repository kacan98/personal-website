## Good first projects

Examples of useful automation for accounting firms:

- automatic reminder flows for missing client documents or approvals
- recurring job creation with prefilled tasks, deadlines, and owners
- invoice follow-up sequences that stop when a payment is registered
- spreadsheet cleanup helpers that prepare imported data before review
