## Good first projects

Examples of useful automation for agencies and consultancies:

- a won-deal handoff flow that creates the right tasks, docs, and kickoff checklist
- an approval flow for content, design, estimates, or delivery sign-off
- a reporting helper that assembles client-ready drafts from messy source data
- an internal AI assistant for recurring research or first-draft preparation
